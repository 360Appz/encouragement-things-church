What Is
SoulQuest?

🧭

The Architecture

Based on the "We're Not Really Strangers" game structure by Koreen Odiney — three progressive levels of Perception, Connection, and Reflection — but rooted in Scripture. Each card gives you a Question, a Verse, an Insight, and a Practice.

🧬

Use It Any Way

Solo journaling or devotional. With a friend or partner — take turns. Small group or Bible study. As daily cards — one card, one day. The WNRS structure works because it warms you up then moves into vulnerability. Don't skip to Level 3. Trust the process.

💡

The Goal

Use the Bible not only to know what is true, but to become the kind of person who can live truthfully, lovingly, courageously, and humbly in everyday life. Identity is received before it is achieved.
